My top 3 movies

3 Idiots

It is a hindi film directed by Raj Hirani. It is a film about life in college and after. It is a film that shows the reality of a student life. If you are a student, this film is a must watch.

Anukokunda Oka Roju

This is a Telugu film directed by Chandrasekhar Yeleti.If you are into psychological thrillers, this film is the perfect pick for you. It is not just a thriller, but gives an amazing perspective on the way we look at life.

Godavari

It is again a Telugu film directed by Shekahar Kammula. This film feels like a warm hug. This is the best comfort film to go for.
